Hey! I'm Rock A Goth! Happy to meet you!

I'm RockAGoth but my name is Christina. I'm from Boston originally but moved out to Washington State back in 2016. I've been gaming for a while with my first system being an NES. My first game was super mario brothers with duck hunt and track and field! It came with the power pad and the gun!

Gaming has meant a lot to me and has gotten me through some of the toughest times in my life. This is one big reason I love streaming retro games. Just hearing and seeing the nostalgia and how it brings back fond memories for people really warms me.
